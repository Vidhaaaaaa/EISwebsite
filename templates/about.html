About Us

The story behind EIS MUJ and our mission

EIS MUJ is more than just a club – we're a community of builders, creators, and innovators who believe in the power of turning ideas into reality. Founded at Manipal University Jaipur, we've created a space where students can experiment, learn, and grow together.

Our approach is simple: focus on action over theory, prototypes over presentations, and community over competition. We believe the best way to learn entrepreneurship is by actually doing it.

Whether you're a developer, designer, writer, or someone with a curious mind, you'll find your place here. We welcome all skill levels and backgrounds – the only requirement is the desire to build something meaningful.
